Website Design Concept

I chose Video Art as the theme of this assignment. The entire website is mainly simple and clear. Because the video pattern is too complex, I want to simplify the design of the entire web page so that the video will not steal the focus or make the web page cluttered.

The color of the top button bar will change when it is moved. Selecting different buttons will go to different websites (window.open), and when typing "yes I do" in the lower right corner, you will go to YouTube. The most difficult interaction on the entire website is boucing ball. I watched a lot of YouTube videos and other people's coding on github, but it couldn't be directly substituted into my file and required a lot of adjustments.

Crafting a seamless bouncing ball animation is challenging due to the intricacies involved in managing spatial dynamics, synchronization, and coordinate transformations within the codebase. For instance, how to set the Ball properties.
